Tirupati

A city famous for its Venkateswara Temple, offering rich culture and religious significance.

Details

Description: Tirupati is a famous pilgrimage city located in Andhra Pradesh, known for the renowned Lord Venkateswara Temple located in Tirumala.

Entry Fee: Free (for general entry)

Best Time to Visit: October to March

Attractions:

  • Lord Venkateswara Temple
  • Kapila Theertham
  • Chandragiri Fort
  • Tirumala Hills

How to Reach: Well connected by road, train, and air (nearest airport is Tirupati Airport).
